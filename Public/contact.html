Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
You can create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button on our homepage. Choose between a job seeker or employer account and follow the registration process.
Is TalentSync free to use?
Yes, creating an account and searching for jobs is completely free for job seekers. Employers have access to basic posting features for free, with premium options available.
How do I post a job?
Employers can post jobs by creating an employer account, accessing their dashboard, and clicking "Post New Job." Fill out the job details and publish your listing.
Can I edit my resume after uploading?
Yes, you can update your resume and profile information at any time through your dashboard. We recommend keeping your information current for the best job matches.
How do I reset my password?
Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.
